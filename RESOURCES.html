Q: Do I need to install anything?

A: No! Just open index.html in your browser. No server, no dependencies, no setup needed.

Q: Is it mobile responsive?

A: Yes, 100% responsive! Works perfectly on all devices from mobile to desktop.

Q: Where is data stored?

A: All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. It persists across sessions until cleared.

Q: Can I customize the design?

A: Absolutely! All CSS is in assets/styles.css and easy to customize. Change colors, fonts, layouts as needed.

Q: Is there a backend?

A: No backend for this demo version. It uses localStorage for data. To add a backend, integrate APIs with your preferred backend technology.
